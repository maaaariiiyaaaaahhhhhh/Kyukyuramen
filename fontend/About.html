Our Story

Born from a love of ramen and a belief that everyone deserves a great bowl.

九九 RAMEN 99
Our Mission

Every Bowl, ₱99.
No Compromises.

KyuKyu Ramen 99 started as a late-night idea between two broke college students who were obsessed with ramen but couldn't afford the ₱350+ bowls at mall restaurants. We thought: why can't good ramen be accessible?

We spent two years testing broths, sourcing noodles, and perfecting recipes. We discovered that with the right techniques — slow simmering, quality bases, smart sourcing — you can serve restaurant-quality ramen at an impossibly fair price.

Today, KyuKyu delivers hundreds of bowls daily across Metro Manila. Our promise is simple: every bowl costs ₱99, every bowl is made with care, and every customer deserves to feel like they got a great deal — because they did.

"Premium ramen for everyone. Not just those who can afford it."
